# How to Create a Work Plan

A work plan is a proactive intelligent project collaboration capability provided by **KWeaver DIP**. It helps users break long-term goals into executable tasks and achieve those goals efficiently through autonomous decision-making by a Digital Worker, automated task progression, progress reporting, and confirmation at key milestones. This document explains how to create and manage a work plan with ease.

## Steps to Create a Work Plan

1. **Open a Digital Worker conversation**
   In the left navigation bar of **KWeaver DIP**, find **My Digital Workers**, select a Digital Worker as needed, and start a conversation.

2. **Enter your goal**
   In the chat box, describe your requirement and goal in natural language. For example:
   - `Learn data analysis within two weeks`
   - `Improve my presentation skills within six months`
   - `Launch a product from 0 to 1 within 3 months`

3. **Confirm the plan**
   The Digital Worker starts analyzing and breaking down your goal. If the goal is not clear enough, it asks follow-up questions through multiple rounds of conversation. After all details are confirmed, the Digital Worker generates a detailed execution roadmap for your review. Once confirmed, click **Generate Plan** to formally create and start the work plan.

## Core Features of a Work Plan

- **Intelligent goal breakdown**: the Digital Worker breaks long-term goals into executable phased tasks based on your needs, ensuring that the plan is clear and actionable.
- **Automated task progression**: the Digital Worker automatically advances task execution without requiring manual intervention, saving time and improving efficiency.
- **Milestone-based progress reporting**: at key milestones, the Digital Worker periodically generates progress reports and synchronizes work progress, potential risks, and next steps.
- **Interactive alignment**: the Digital Worker proactively communicates with you for confirmation, ensuring that execution stays aligned with your expectations and that the plan can be adjusted in time.

## Work Plan Management

### View a Work Plan

You can find work plans through either of the following entry points:

- Entry 1: In the left navigation bar of **KWeaver DIP**, open **My Digital Workers**. After clicking a specific Digital Worker, you can view all work plans created under that Digital Worker.
- Entry 2: In the left navigation bar of **KWeaver DIP**, locate the **Work Plan** section and click **More** to enter the **Work Plan** list. On that page, you can view different plans created by different Digital Workers.

After locating a work plan, click it to open the detail page and review its execution history and outputs.

### Modify a Work Plan

Open the detail page of the work plan and enter a specific modification instruction in the chat box, such as `Adjust the goal of Phase 3 to ...`. After the Digital Worker confirms the change, the work plan is updated.

### Pause or Cancel a Work Plan

1. **Pause a plan**: type `Pause the plan` in the chat box, or click the **...** button and choose **Pause** from the dropdown menu.
2. **Cancel a plan**: type `Cancel the plan`, and then choose **Pause Only** or **Confirm Termination**.
