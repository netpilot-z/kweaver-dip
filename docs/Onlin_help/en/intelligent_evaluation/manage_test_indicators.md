# Manage Test Indicators

## Overview

### Feature Definition

Test indicators are standardized metrics used to quantify the performance, quality, or effectiveness of the evaluated target in Benchmark scenarios. They are the basis for comparative analysis and objective judgment of test results.

## Steps

1. Log in to ADP and choose **Test Indicators** under the **Intelligent Evaluation** module from the left navigation bar.
   - **Function Calculation**: evaluates the performance of models, custom applications, and similar targets through specific quantitative metrics such as keyword match rate and ROUGE score. These indicators are usually based on predefined standards and algorithms, can provide objective numerical results, but may not fully reflect real-world effectiveness.
   - **AI Evaluation**: uses advanced language models to simulate human evaluators and automatically score the quality of answers. It is intended to provide a fast and automated evaluation approach while maintaining a certain level of accuracy and objectivity.
2. On the **Test Indicators** page, click **Upload** in the upper-right corner. You can upload the required indicator file based on the **sample template** provided on the page.
3. After the file is uploaded, you can directly view the following on the current page:
   - Indicator usage instructions
   - Parameter indicators
   - File preview
